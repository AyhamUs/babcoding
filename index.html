Welcome to the Bab Coding Group! We're a passionate community of student developers dedicated to making technology accessible to everyone. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing high-quality, professional website development services completely free of charge to local businesses, non-profits, and individuals who might not otherwise have access to professional web development.
